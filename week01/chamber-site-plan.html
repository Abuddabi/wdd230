WDD 230

Site Name:

CHAMBER OF COMMERCE in Russia, Saint-Petersburg

This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the website is to serve as an online hub for the Chamber of Commerce in Russia,Saint-Petersburg. Through this platform, businesses, entrepreneurs, and stakeholders can access valuable resources, information, and networking opportunities to foster economic growth, collaboration, and success in the region.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
