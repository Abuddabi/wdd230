Demographics of Saint-Petersburg

Saint Petersburg is the second largest city in Russia and the fourth most populous city in Europe.

Population of Saint-Petersburg is about 5 million people.

Interesting facts about the city

  • Venice of the North: Saint Petersburg is often referred to as the "Venice of the North" due to its extensive network of canals and more than 300 bridges.
  • Hermitage Museum: The Hermitage Museum, located in the Winter Palace, is one of the largest and oldest museums in the world. It houses an extensive collection of art and artifacts spanning thousands of years and includes works by renowned artists such as Leonardo da Vinci, Rembrandt, and Michelangelo.
  • White Nights: Saint Petersburg is famous for its "White Nights," a phenomenon occurring during the summer months when the sun does not fully set, resulting in a twilight that lasts throughout the night.
